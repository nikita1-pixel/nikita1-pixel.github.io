About Me

From behind the counter to behind the data.

I'm a BCA graduate (2026, MIT-WPU Pune) and a self-driven Data Analyst. I spent 2.5 years as a Starbucks barista reading customers in real time — now I read what their data is trying to say.

I work the full analyst loop: ask the right question → clean the messy data → analyse it → build the dashboard → tell the story. I've cleaned and analysed 1.3M real loan records, shipped a live web app, and built executive dashboards in Power BI and Tableau.

I care about honest analysis — insights you can actually act on, not vanity charts.
